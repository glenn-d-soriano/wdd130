Winnipeg

Winnipeg is the capital and largest city in the Canadian province of Manitoba. Winnipeg is located at the confluence of the Red and Assiniboine rivers, about 100 km north of the Minnesota border. It's near the geographic center of North America and midway between the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans. Winnipeg is known for being a multicultural city with over 100 languages and nationalities. Its unique winter experiences, arts scene, festivals, and character neighborhoods. It is also home to the Royal Winnipeg Ballet, the Winnipeg Philharmonic Choir, and the Winnipeg Folk Festival
